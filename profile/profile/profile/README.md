Hi there, this is Appy Pie Automate 👋

[Appy Pie Automate](https://appypieautomate.ai) is a **no-code workflow automation platform** that connects **1,000+ apps** — letting anyone build powerful automated workflows without writing a single line of code.

### What we're building

We're on a mission to make automation accessible to everyone. With Appy Pie Automate, your marketing, sales, ops, and support teams can automate their own workflows in minutes — no developer required.

- ⚡ **1,000+ integrations** — Gmail, Slack, Shopify, HubSpot, Salesforce, Stripe, Trello, Notion, and more
- 🧩 **No-code visual builder** — Connect apps with a drag-and-drop workflow editor
- 🤖 **AI-powered automation** — Smart triggers, filters, and actions powered by AI
- 🔒 **Enterprise-ready** — SOC 2 compliant, GDPR-ready, 99.9% uptime SLA
- 🌍 **Global scale** — Millions of tasks automated every day across 150+ countries
